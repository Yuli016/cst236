Controls

D-Pad: Moves Link left and right, up and down as this is a top-view game. Also moves the inventory cursor.
A Button: Swings your sword when when you push this button. Also used to confirm a selection.
B Button: Pushing this button will make you use whichever item is currently equipped. You can equip an item to the B button by selecting it in the inventory.
Select: Selects options and also pauses the game.
Start: Also able to pause the game as well as bringing up the subscreen.

Controls

The Legend of Zelda
Ocarina Of Time
Skyward Sword

Controls

A Button: Action, Take, Climb Down
B Button: Sprint, Close
X Button: Jump, Climb
Y Button: Attack
R Stick: Move camera, telescope view (click)
L Stick: Move Link, stealth mode (click)
L: Use item, navigate menu
ZL: Shield/Lock on target (hold)
R: Throw weapon, navigate menu
ZR: Bow (draw and shoot arrows)
◄: Switch shield (hold and change with right stick)
►: Switch weapon (hold and change with right stick)
▲: Switch special item (hold and change with right stick)
▼: Whistle
-: Sheikah Slate (runes, powers, map, missions, destinations, memories)
+: Pause/inventory (weapons, armor, gear)
◘: Capture Button: Capture screenshot/video
⌂: Home Button
Tilt Switch/controller: Precision aiming
